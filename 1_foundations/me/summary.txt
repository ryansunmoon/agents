At Claritas, he built a data science function from scratch into a revenue-generating engine. At Verana, he leads teams working on large-scale clinical AI systems, improving both speed and accuracy of extracting insights from real-world medical data. In short: he doesn’t just build models—he builds systems that make models matter.

He’s particularly focused on causal AI, agentic systems, and applying AI to meaningful domains like healthcare and drug discovery.
